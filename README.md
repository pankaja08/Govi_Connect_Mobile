**Govi Connect** is an all-in-one mobile solution designed to modernize traditional farming. Developed for the **Web and Mobile Technologies (WMT)** module at SLIIT, this platform integrates AI diagnostics, community-driven knowledge sharing, and administrative governance to support the agricultural lifecycle.

---

## 📱 Core App Modules

### 📚 Content Management (Knowledge Hub)
* **Expert Blogs:** A central repository for agricultural articles and research.
* **Rich Media:** Seamlessly integrated images and guides to help farmers stay updated on modern techniques.

### 🤝 Community Interaction
* **Farmer Forum:** Engage in discussions, share experiences, and seek advice.
* **Interactive Features:** Read, comment, and filter posts based on specific agricultural interests to find relevant information quickly.

### 🛡️ Admin Management Dashboard
*The central nervous system for platform governance:*
* **User & Expert Control:** Manage registrations and verify agricultural experts.
* **Ad Moderation:** Review and oversee marketplace advertisements for quality assurance.
* **System Oversight:** Monitor platform health and generate analytical reports.

### 💡 Smart Crop Advisory System
* **Personalized Recommendations:** Get crop suggestions based on location, current season, and soil type.
* **Care Instructions:** Access detailed guidelines for irrigation, fertilizer usage, and common pest prevention.

### 🛒 Marketplace
* **Buy & Sell:** A dedicated space for agricultural products, seeds, and tools.
* **Direct Access:** Empowering local sellers by removing unnecessary middlemen.

### 📅 Personal Farming Workspace
* **Secure Access:** Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) for Users, Experts, and Admins.
* **Activity Tracker:** A digital farming calendar to schedule tasks, monitor growth cycles, and mark seasonal milestones.

| **Authentication** | JWT (JSON Web Tokens) |

---

## 🏗️ System Architecture
The application follows a **MERN stack** architecture, utilizing **Cloudinary** for efficient cloud-based image management, ensuring that high-resolution blog images and marketplace items do not compromise app performance.
